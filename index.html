Low-dimensional Audio Inpainting with mixed generative-predictive model

This page contains some listening samples to evaluate the performance of LDAI, a mixed generative-predictive DNN framework aimed at repairing very long duration corrupted audio fragments in musical sequences. The proposed pipeline incorporates a convolutional encoder-decoder structure for timbre mapping, working in time domain, and a low-level conditional WGAN for "semantic" inpainting, working at low-level. The motivation for splitting the process into a predictive task and a generative task, is to reduce the effort required by the latter, thus ensuring robust and fast training convergence while providing richer musical note generation.
